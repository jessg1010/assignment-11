Why Lady Gaga?

Lady Gaga is an American pop singer whose real name is Stephanie Johanna Angelina Germanotta. I enjoy listening to her music because it is very upbeat and catchy. Unlike many artists, her music is not repetitive, and her lyrics span beyond cliches. I also think she is an interesting performer and would love to see her live.
